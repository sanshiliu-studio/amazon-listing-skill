# Category Writing Examples

Use these examples as style guidance, not fixed templates. Match the user's actual product, proof, and category.

## Clothing, Shoes, and Jewelry

Title pattern:

```text
Brand Women's Summer Maxi Dress, A-Line Short Sleeve
```

Item highlight ideas:

```text
fabric, silhouette, neckline, sleeve style, occasion, pockets, care instructions
```

Bullet themes:

- Size and fit.
- Fabric feel and season.
- Neckline, sleeve, waist, pocket, or closure details.
- Use scenarios such as beach, travel, work, vacation, daily wear, party, or wedding guest.
- Care instructions and fit notes.

## Electronics

Title pattern:

```text
Brand 20W USB C Charger for iPhone 17/16/15, 2-Pack
```

Item highlight ideas:

```text
wattage, charging port, included cable, compact design, compatible devices
```

Bullet themes:

- Compatibility with devices.
- Charging speed only when technically supportable.
- Built-in protection features only when provided by the user.
- Included pieces.
- Travel, desk, office, or home use.

## Cell Phones and Accessories

Title pattern:

```text
Brand for iPhone Model Case with MagSafe, Color
```

Item highlight ideas:

```text
Material, protection, MagSafe, scratch resistance, grip/design
```

Bullet themes:

- Exact model fit.
- Cutouts, button response, and camera coverage.
- Materials, grip, scratch resistance, and everyday protection.
- MagSafe or wireless charging only when true.
- Warranty or support only when provided by the user.

## Home and Kitchen

Title pattern:

```text
Brand Queen Mattress Topper, Bamboo Cooling Pillow Top
```

Item highlight ideas:

```text
size, material, comfort layer, fit depth, care, room use
```

Bullet themes:

- Set contents, size, capacity, or included pieces.
- Materials and construction.
- Comfort, insulation, cooling, storage, or cleaning benefit.
- Ease of use and care.
- Fit notes and measurement caveats.

## General Product Types

When no category example fits, use this structure:

- Bullet 1: exact product identity and set contents.
- Bullet 2: material, construction, or technical spec.
- Bullet 3: primary shopper benefit.
- Bullet 4: use scenario and fit.
- Bullet 5: care, installation, compatibility, or verification note.
