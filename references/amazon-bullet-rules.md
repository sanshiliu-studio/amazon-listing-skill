# Amazon Bullet Point Rules

Use these rules when drafting or auditing Amazon bullet points.

Primary reference: Amazon Seller Central Product bullet point requirements.

## Required Shape

- Include at least five bullet points unless the user asks for a different count.
- Keep each bullet point between 10 and 255 characters.
- Begin each bullet point with a capital letter.
- Use a short header followed by a colon, then a concise description.
- Write as a sentence fragment. Do not use final punctuation.
- Use semicolons to separate phrases inside one bullet point when needed.
- Include a space between number and unit, such as `60 ml`.
- Write numbers one to nine in full, except in names, model numbers, pack counts, measurements, and technical specs.

## Content Rules

- Highlight features, benefits, use cases, fit, included pieces, material, care, and shopper decision points.
- Keep claims factual and supportable.
- Avoid brand backstory unless it directly helps the shopper choose the product.
- Avoid duplicating the title, product overview, or product attributes word for word.
- Keep variant data consistent across color, size, pack, or style variants.
- Do not compare the product to competitor brands.
- Do not divert shoppers to other products not included under the ASIN.

## Claim Caution

Treat these as risky unless the user provides packaging, certificate, test, manual, or legal proof:

- Medical, health, therapeutic, antimicrobial, anti-allergy, pain relief, or disease-related claims.
- Safety certification claims.
- `military-grade`, `drop-proof`, `waterproof`, `fireproof`, or similar performance claims.
- `best`, `fastest`, `cheapest`, `number one`, `top rated`, or sales/ranking claims.
- Environmental claims such as `eco friendly`, `biodegradable`, or `sustainable`.

## Style Patterns

Good bullet:

```text
Soft fabric: Made from lightweight breathable material for comfortable daily wear
```

Good technical bullet:

```text
Fast charging: Delivers 20W USB C power for compatible phones, tablets, and earbuds
```

Risky bullet:

```text
Best quality: The most amazing charger on Amazon with guaranteed fastest charging
```
